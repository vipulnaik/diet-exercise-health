# Non-strength day exercise cycle (with history)

## Trigger for execution of non-strength day exercises

In an ideal world, where I don't skip exercise on any day, I do
hanging strength exercises every 3 days, and weightlifting/push-up
strength exercises every 3 days. This leaves the third day of the
3-day cycle, where I aim to *not* do strength exercises, thereby
supporting recovery.

I use these non-strength days for various other exercises. Choosing
the non-strength days for these exercises roughly evens out the time
footprint of exercises across days.

The time of the non-strength exercise within the day depends on the
exercise. For some exercises, I do them before jogging; timing-wise,
it's just before closing for, or getting ready to close for,
jogging. Others I do after jogging.

Most but not all of the exercises are focused on some sort of breath
control.

## Cycle time

If I never skip exercise, the cycle time is about 9 days.

In practice, the cycle time is closer to 15 days.

## Cycle exercises and occasional exercises

### Cycle exercises

* [Breath holds](breath-holds-with-history.md) (before jogging but can also be done at any time later in the day, just not immediately after jogging)

* [Humming with intermittent pauses](humming-with-intermittent-pauses-with-history.md) (before jogging but can also be done at any time later in the day, just not immediately after jogging)

* [Deliberate falling practice](deliberate-falling-practice-with-history.md) (after jogging)

### Occasional exercises

* [Incentive spirometry](incentive-spirometry-with-history.md) (before jogging but can also be done at any time later in the day, just not immediately after jogging)

* [Vocal note holds](vocal-note-holds-with-history.md) (before jogging)
